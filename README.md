# youtube-custom-loop
Firefox extension to loop multiple YouTube video segments sequentially
Whether you want to loop a single favorite section of a video or queue up multiple specific segments to play in sequence, this add-on makes it effortless. No more manual scrubbing or constant clicking.

🌟 Key Features:

Multi-Segment Looping: Add as many segments as you want using the "+" button. The player will automatically cycle through them in chronological order.

Instant Timestamp Capture (📍): Forget manual typing! Just click the pin icon next to the input fields to instantly grab the current video time.

Native Integration: The "Multi-Loop" button blends seamlessly into the YouTube toolbar, right next to the native Likes/Dislikes buttons, keeping your page clean and uncluttered.

Privacy-First: Operates 100% locally in your browser. Absolutely no data collection, no tracking, and no external servers.

🎧 Perfect For:

ASMR & Relaxation: Seamlessly loop your favorite relaxing triggers and skip loud talking, intros, or transitions.

Music & Playlists: Loop that amazing guitar solo, a specific song bridge, or repeat a section of a live performance.

Study & Tutorials: Replay complex steps in programming, digital art, or crafting tutorials until you master them.

Language Learning: Repeat dialogues or pronunciation exercises over and over without touching your mouse.

🛠 How to use:

Open any YouTube video.

Click the native Multi-Loop button next to the Like/Dislike buttons.

Use the 📍 pin button to automatically set your start and end times, or type them manually (format: MM:SS or HH:MM:SS).

Click "+" to add more segments if desired.

Hit Start and enjoy your custom loop! Click Stop at any time to return to normal playback.
